Pure black (#09090b) background, zinc borders, extremely sparse. Command palette (⌘K). Geometric nav icons. No colour except status indicators and the gradient accent. Feels like a developer tool, not a business app.

B — Data-Dense with Charts (Datadog/Grafana)

Icon-only sidebar (collapsed). Top bar with time range + filters. Prominent chart area above stats. Trend indicators (↑ ↓) on every metric. More data visible at once. For power users who live in the dashboard.
